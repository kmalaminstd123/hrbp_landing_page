Understanding HRBP

What is a Human Resource Business Partner?

An HRBP is a strategic HR professional who works closely with business leaders to align human resource initiatives with organizational objectives.

Unlike traditional HR roles focused on administrative tasks, HRBPs act as consultants and change agents, bridging the gap between HR strategy and business execution. They understand both the people and business sides of the organization.

Key Responsibilities:

Strategic Planning

Align HR strategies with business goals

Talent Management

Develop and retain top talent

Organizational Development

Drive culture and change initiatives

Recertification Process

Why Recertification?

To ensure you maintain current knowledge, skills, and align with evolving HR and business trends. Recertification demonstrates your commitment to continuous professional development and keeps your credentials relevant in the dynamic HR landscape.

Recertification Cycle

3 Years

Your certification must be renewed every 3 years to maintain its validity and ensure your knowledge remains current with industry standards.

Recertification Methods

Participants can renew their certification through one of the following:

Continuing Education

Earn 36 hours of professional involvement via:

  • Attending webinars
  • Completing advanced HR courses
  • Participating in industry conferences
  • Facilitating relevant training
  • Attending any consultancy in relevant field

Assessment-Based Renewal

Complete a recertification assessment that includes:

  • Assignment-based evaluation
  • Case study analysis
  • Testing understanding of updated HR practices
  • Recent business case studies

Recertification Fee

NO FEE

There is no fee required for recertification. We believe in supporting your continuous professional development without financial barriers.

Deliverables Upon Renewal

Certificate Verification

Verify your renewed certificate through our secure online portal

Renewed Digital Badge

Receive your updated digital badge via Credly to showcase on LinkedIn and other platforms
